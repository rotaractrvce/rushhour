Akshara 4.0

About

Akshara- An event planned and started to truly help the rural kids in a way which cannot be done by donating material things or money. The main motive of ‘Akshara’ was to promote and create awareness about the personality development skills, current education scenario and moreover the basic ideas of life among the kids in the rural schools which they did not have access to or which is mostly ignored. The Rotaract club of RVCE conducted this event for the first time in the Rota year 2017-18 and it was tremendously successful. The event was conducted across 7 schools in Bangalore with 500 students involved. The idea was to send the volunteers to these schools and teach the students there. The teaching process was well thought upon and a proper curriculum was devised to be followed during the event. Our volunteers had done a fabulous job of contacting the schools for prior permission and convincing them of our honest idea and vision towards a more productive step for the society. The club was extremely grateful for the trust bestowed upon us by the schools. The curriculum involved basic maths, English speaking skills, interactive sessions and personality development skills. Two to three volunteers took charge of a class and managed it perfectly fine. The love and response the volunteers received on spot from the students and the school management was overwhelming. This event was highly beneficial for our volunteers as well. They got a chance to interact with these kids and contribute to this society in such a manner. Looking at the resounding success of the event, the Rotaract club of RVCE looks forward to conduct this event at a larger scale than last year.
