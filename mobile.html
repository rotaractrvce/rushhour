Who are we?

Rotaract RVCE is one of the most oldest and dynamic clubs in Rvce. We are a group of enthusiastic individuals who strive ahead for bringing about a positive change in our society. We work under our parent Rotary Club-3190 Our Mottos are as follows:

  1. 1.To develop professional Skills in an individual.

  2. 2.To inculcate leadership skills, through leading events and organizing of large scale projects within or outside the college.

  3. 3.To realize the innate talent and ability residing in every individual and help him/her come to terms with the full range of his potential.

  4. 4.To recognize, practice and promote ethical standards as management qualities and vocational responsibilities.
